Youth Expedition Project (YEP) in Singapore is a flagship initiative under the National Youth Council aimed at nurturing a sense of social responsibility, global awareness, and leadership skills among young Singaporeans through overseas community service projects. Participants, typically students or young adults, collaborate with local communities in countries across Asia to address pressing issues such as education, health, water sanitation, and sustainable livelihoods. These projects often involve hands-on activities like teaching, infrastructure building, or providing essential resources, fostering meaningful cross-cultural exchanges and empathy for diverse global challenges.
